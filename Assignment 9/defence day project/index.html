Pakistan's Defence Day

Significance of 6th September

Defence Day is observed annually on 6th September in Pakistan to honor the country's military forces and to commemorate their valor during the 1965 Indo-Pak War. On this day, the nation celebrates the bravery and sacrifices of the armed forces and the unity of the people.

"We will stand united and resolute against any aggression. The strength of Pakistan's defence lies in its people and their unwavering spirit." - Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah
